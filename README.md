# Bachelor_Thesis
My driver-off road thesis titled "CLASH: Development of an Intelligent Traffic Management System: Drive Off-Road".
The main aim of my thesis was to connect people, mainly non-technical users, with remote-driven vehicles by enabling real-time vehicle control using a joystick.

The thesis explored connecting remote-driven vehicles and their modules with a joystick to allow further understanding and trust between the general public and remote-driven vehicles. This was accomplished through the cooperation and collaboration with other ADAS and Cooperative Control students as part of the major CLASH project under the grand supervision of the Multi-Robot Systems (MRS) Research Group.
